About Meridian Tech

Meridian Tech is led by CEO S Anthoni Swamy and specialises in advanced CNC cutting tools for milling, drilling, turning and finishing. Our mission is to provide industrial partners with tooling that minimises cycle time and maximises component quality. We combine modern coating technologies, precision geometry and rigorous quality control to deliver tools that stand up to heavy production demands.

Products

High-quality tools for every stage of machining

End Mills & Milling Cutters

Carbide end mills with special geometries for roughing, finishing and slotting across a range of materials.

Drills & Reamers

High-performance drills and reamers designed for precision holes, long tool life and stable chip evacuation.

Indexable Inserts & Toolholders

Premium grade inserts and rigid toolholder systems for turning and milling operations.

Cutting Inserts (Ceramic / CBN)

Specialised inserts for hard turning and high-speed finishing — ceramic and CBN grades available.

Custom Tooling Solutions

Bespoke tooling design and small-batch manufacturing to meet unique production challenges.

Tool Reconditioning & Technical Support

Regrinding, recoating and technical consultation to extend tool life and optimise processes.
